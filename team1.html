Renata Ona

As a producer and actor, Renata is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP), a published academic author, and has her Master's in International Affairs. Her improvisational and acting abilities, as well as voice acting skills continue to contribute to Hamilton's fringe and community theatre landscapes, as well as the world of background acting.

In addition to founding her own company, Hiccup Productions, she has worked with theatre companies such as The Players' Guild of Hamilton, Emerson Arts, 9M Theatre, Reactor Theatre, and Maple Leaf Productions; choral organizations such as The Harlequin Singers and Southern Ontario Lyric Opera; and background recruitment organizations such as GHA Talent and Bon-Ker Casting.
