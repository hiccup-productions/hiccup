For What We Labour

Set in Ancient Greece, this original comedy and "kitchen sink drama" follows Anthisma, her father (Aplistos), their housekeeper (Lamprosa), her best friend (Evrostos), and two untimely guests (Pyknos and Paratolmos). And is narrated by Hamartia, an exiled member of The Greek Chorus.

Showtime 23 November 2025

Liz, A Traumatic Comedy

We're proud to be partnering with 9MTheatre for the 2025 Hamilton Fringe Festival. Catch Liz, a Traumatic Comedy this summer! Sometimes vulnerable, but mostly silly, Liz tells her mental health journey from manic pixie dream girl to depressed pixie dream girl, to reasonably well-adjusted adult.

Showtime Hamilton Fringe Festival, July 17-27, 2025
