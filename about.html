About Hiccup Productions

Creativity shares characteristics with the humble hiccup. By one definition, a hiccup is a tiny, involuntary reflex usually brought on by strong emotion such as laughter or fear. By another definition, it is a wee mistake. We embrace both definitions in our company ethos, because they yield great things - and so do you.

Launced in 2025, Hiccup Productions is born and raised in Hamilton. We love the act of blending theatre and creativity with movement and well-being.

We specialize in play, fun, and comedy. But in the true spirit of awe, continual inspiration, and making mistakes, we'll try anything!
