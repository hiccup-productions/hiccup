About Hiccup Productions

We love the humble hiccup. It's a tiny, involuntary reflex, usually brought on by laughter or fear. It can be a wee mistake. The hiccup yields great things.

While officially launched in 2025, Hiccup Productions was born and raised in Hamilton. We blend theatre with movement and well-being.
