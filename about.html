About Hiccup Productions

Started in 2025, Hiccup Productions is a Hamilton-based, creative company. While relatively new, we have fresh eyes and over 10 years of project management experience to bring to the table. The puzzle of theatre production fits very nicely into our wheelhouse. Plus, we love the creative jumble of ideas, personalities, emotions, urgency, and demands of the theatre and the small screen.

From writing, to acting, to producing, to teaching - we specialize in play, fun, and comedy. But in the true spirit of awe, continual inspiration, and making mistakes, we'll try anything!

Why "Hiccup" though? Fair enough. We celebrate that creativity shares characteristics with the humble hiccup. By one definition, a hiccup is a tiny, involuntary reflex usually brought on by strong emotion such as laughter or fear. By another definition, it is a wee mistake. And we embrace both definitions in our company ethos, because they yield great things.

We grew up in Hamilton, explored away from Hamilton, returned to Hamilton, and now we intend now to grow in the fruitful soils of Hamilton's arts and culture scene.
