About Hiccup Productions

Founded by Renata Ona, a Hamilton-based producer, writer, and actor, Hiccup Productions was officially launched in 2025. Renata is a Master's-educated and well-rounded creative professional, skilled in creative direction and project management. She has the unique experience of having worked in the fast-paced world of corporate project management before embarking on a journey to entertain wide-ranging audiences with quintessentially Canadian humour and pride.

Renata's improvisational prowess, dynamic acting ability, and voice acting skills richly contribute to Hamilton's Fringe Theatre and community theatre landscapes. Having worked with companies such as The Player's Guild of Hamilton, Emerson Arts, 9M Theatre, Reactor Theatre, and Maple Leaf Productions. She is a rising force in Hamilton's theatre scene.

Having worked with organizations such as Theatre Aquarius, FirstOntario Concert Hall, Hamilton Theatre Inc., the Hamilton Fringe Festival, Cobalt Connects and The City of Hamilton, Hiccup Productions is rooted in community connections.

And in case you're curious - we love the humble hiccup. It's a tiny, involuntary reflex, usually brought on by laughter or fear. Inevitably, it yields great things.
