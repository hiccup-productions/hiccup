About Hiccup Productions

Hiccup Productions was founded by Renata Ona, a Hamilton-based producer, writer, and actor. Renata is a Master's-educated and well-rounded creative professional, skilled in creative direction and project management. She has the unique experience of working in fast-paced corporate project management for ten years before entertaining audiences with quintessentially Canadian humour and pride.

The Hiccup Productions team is deeply rooted in the Hamilton community and built through proven collaboration and project execution. Renata has worked with organizations such as IATSE Local 129 and Cobalt Connects, on projects for Theatre Aquarius, FirstOntario Concert Hall, and the City of Hamilton. In addition, she's worked and volunteered for the Toronto International Film Festival, Hamilton Theatre Inc., Hamilton's gritLIT Festival, and the Hamilton Fringe Festival.

The company is founded on love for the humble hiccup - a tiny, involuntary reflex, usually brought on by laughter or fear. Inevitably, it yields great things.

For inquiries or collaboration opportunities, please contact us via email.

Acting

As a producer and actor, Renata is a well-rounded professional. Her improvisational and acting abilities, as well as voice acting skills continue to contribute to Hamilton's fringe and community theatre landscapes as well as the world of background acting. She has worked with theatre companies such as The Player's Guild of Hamilton, Emerson Arts, 9M Theatre, Reactor Theatre, and Maple Leaf Productions; choral organizations such as The Harlequin Singers and Southern Ontario Lyric Opera; and background recruitment organizations such as GHA Talent and Bon-Ker Casting.
