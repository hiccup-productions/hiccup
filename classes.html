Drop-In Information

  • Class is led by a certified instructor.
  • Please book online. Each class costs $18 and lasts 50-55 minutes.
  • Classes are suitable for beginners to advanced movers.
  • Classes are an inclusive, safe space for ALL adults to move and creatively express*.
  • For questions, please email: movewithrenata@gmail.com
  • Parking is available.

*Right now, The Staircase - Bright Room is only accessible by stairs. But we are actively looking for additional space that would suit a greater range of accessibility needs. Maybe it'll be called The Elevator.
