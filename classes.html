About Movement Mornings

Movement Mornings @ The Staircase are an intentional blending of the fun and fitness for adults. Join in for interactive, play-based warm-ups followed by a foundational movement, bodyweight workout. Classes range in intensity but each can be modified to your skill level. Start your day with play!

Instructor

Renata is a local Hamilton mover and creative, certified in adult gymnastics, assisted stretching, pilates, and personal training. She studied public health in university. And participants in her classes will additionally benefit from her training in theatre, improv, singing, dance, and more.

Drop-In Information

  • Please book online. Drop-Ins are $18 inclusive and last 50-55 minutes.
  • Classes are an inclusive, safe space for ALL adults.
  • There is no elevator up to The Staircase Bright Room. But we are seeking additional space for accessibility needs. Maybe it'll be called The Elevator.
  • Free parking is available.
  • For questions, please email: movewithrenata@gmail.com
