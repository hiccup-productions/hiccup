About Movement Mornings

Movement Mornings blend pilates, improv, and skill-building for adults. Join in for play-based warm-ups followed by a foundational movement, bodyweight workout. Each class can be modified to your skill level. Start your day with play!

Instructor

Renata is a local Hamilton mover and creative, certified in adult gymnastics, assisted stretching, pilates, and personal training. She studied public health in university. And participants in her classes will additionally benefit from her training in theatre, improv, singing, dance, and more.

Drop-In Information

  • Drop-Ins are a sliding scale of $12-$15 inclusive and last 50-55 minutes.
  • Classes are an inclusive, safe space for ALL adults.
  • For questions, please email: movewithrenata@gmail.com
