Hiccup Productions is a stage and television production company based in Hamilton ON.

Founded by producer, writer, and actor Renata Ona, Hiccup Productions offers exceptional services and accessible opportunities to mounting productions.

Services include playwriting, copyediting, public relations, event management, and creative direction.

Hiccup Productions is a rising force in the Canadian production landscape.
